RETURN POLICY

We want you to get exactly what you ordered. If something is not right, please follow the directions below to make a return within 7 days of receiving your purchase. Items returned must be new, unopened, and in their original packaging. Open box returns on non-defective items will not be accepted.

RETURN PROCESS

1. Please email us at support@gallantry.com to initiate the return within 7 days of receiving your shipment, including your order number in the subject line. Let us know which items you will be sending back, and include a photo, as well as the reason you are returning the product.

2. Ship the product back to our warehouse (address below), and our support team will be on the lookout for it. The products must be sent undamaged in their original unopened packaging, and must be received at our warehouse within 7 days of initiating the return.

3. Once we receive and verify your return, we will credit back your funds to your original method of payment within 7 business days.
